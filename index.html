Welcome to Moneta - the JSR 354 reference implementation

This module implements JSR 354 Money & Currency. Hereby basically implementations of amounts, currency and roundings are provided. You can access the RI by adding the following maven dependenceies:

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.javamoney</groupId>
  <artifactId>jsr-354-ri</artifactId>
  <version>0.7-SNAPSHOT</version>
</dependency>

Nevertheless we highly recommend you to depend on the Javamoney OSS library to be used, due to the following reasons:

  • the RI only contains a very small, minimalistic subset of features.
  • The JavaMoney project contains additional functionalities, such as ** currency conversion, ** currency management and ** rounding and formatting faatures, ** regions and validite4s support ** bundles for different runtime environments
